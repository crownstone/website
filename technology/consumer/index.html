Our Technology

Crownstone’s indoor positioning hardware uses your mere presence and proximity to control lights and devices in the home or office environment. Crownstone also provides indoor positioning to businesses on its hardware-as-a-platform.

What can Crownstones do for you at home?

Switch

Switch on lights and devices via the app or at set times.

Dimmer

Set your (LED) lighting to a desired dimming position.

Discover more about the dimming function

Energy consumption

View the energy consumption per device in real-time.

Indoor positioning

Crownstones react to someone presence or proximity

Learn more about indoor positioning

Twilight

Automatically dim the lights when it gets dark outside. This even works with your regular light switch.

Switchcraft

Your existing light switches will continue to work!

How accurate can indoor positioning be?



Bluetooth Low Energy positioning can be fairly accurate. It all depends on the number of Crownstones you use! See in this video clip below how we move a smartphone in a miniature house from room to room and you see how the picture moves accordingly.
