Declaration of conformity

You can find the CE certification as pdf. The product complies with the following:

Directives:

  • 2014/53/EU Radio Equipment Directive (RED)

Article 3.1a: Health and Safety

  • EN 62368-1:2016
  • EN 60669-1:2018
  • EN 60669-2-1:2004/A12:2010

Article 3.1b: EMC

  • EN 301 489-1 V2.1.1
  • EN 301 489-17 V3.1.1

Article 3.2: Radio

  • EN 300 328 V2.1.1
